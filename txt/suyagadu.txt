kim āha bandhaṇaṃ vīro | kiṃ vā jāṇaṃ tiuai 
cittamantam acittaṃ vā | parigijjha kisām avi /
annaṃ vā aṇujāṇāi | evaṃ dukkhā na muccaī 
sayaṃ tivāyae pāṇe | aduvannehi ghāyae /
haṇantaṃ vāṇujāṇāi | veraṃ vaḍḍhei appaṇo

ee ganthe viukkamma | ege samaṇamāhaṇā /
ayāṇantā viussittā | sattā kāmehi māṇavā 
santi pañca mahabbhūyā | ihamegesimāhiyā /

patteyaṃ kasiṇe āyā | je bālā je ya paṇḍiyā /

sarīrassa viṇāseṇaṃ | viṇāso hoi dehiṇo

 S/1.2 samayajjhayaṇe paḍhame /
 
āghāyaṃ puṇa egesiṃ | uvavannā puḍho jiyā /
vedayanti suhaṃ dukkhaṃ | adu vā luppanti ṭhāṇao 
na taṃ sayaṃkaḍaṃ dukkhaṃ | kao annakaḍaṃ ca -aṃ /
suhaṃ vā jai vā dukkhaṃ | sehiyaṃ vā asehiyaṃ 
sayaṃkaḍaṃ na annehiṃ | vedayanti puḍho jiyā /
saṃgaiyaṃ taṃ tahā tesiṃ | ihamegesimāhiyaṃ 
evam eyāṇi jampantā | bālā paṇḍiyamāṇiṇo /
niyayāniyayaṃ santaṃ | ayāṇantā abuddhiyā 
evam ege u pāsatthā | te bhujjo vippagabbhiyā /
evaṃ uvaṭhiyā santā | na te dukkhavimokkhagā 
javiṇo migā jahā santā | pariyāṇeṇa vajjiyā /
asaṅkiyāiṃ saṅkanti | saṅkiyāiṃ asaṅkiṇo 
pariyāṇiyāṇi saṅkantā | pāsiyāṇi asaṅkiṇo /
annāṇabhayasaṃviggā | saṃpalinti tahiṃ tahiṃ 
aha taṃ pavejja bajjhaṃ | ahe bajjhassa vā vae /
muccejja payapāsāo | taṃ tu mande na dehaī 
ahiyappāhiyapannāṇe | visaman teṇuvāgae /
sa baddhe payapāseṇaṃ | tattha ghāyaṃ niyacchai 
evaṃ tu samaṇā ege | micchadiṭhī aṇāriyā /
asaṅkiyāiṃ saṅkanti | saṅkiyāiṃ asaṅkiṇo 
dhammapannavaṇā jā sā | taṃ tu saṅkanti mūḍhagā /
ārambhāiṃ na saṅkanti | aviyattā akoviyā 
savvappagaṃ viukkassaṃ | savvaṃ nūmaṃ vihūṇiyā /
appattiyaṃ akammaṃse | eyam aṭhaṃ mige cue 
je eyaṃ nābhijāṇanti | micchadiṭhī aṇāriyā /
migā vā pāsabaddhā te | ghāyam essanti -antaso 
māhaṇā samaṇā ege | savve nāṇaṃ sayaṃ vae /
savvaloge vi je pāṇā | na te jāṇanti kiṃcaṇa 
milakkhū amilakkhussa | jahā vuttāṇubhāsae /
na heuṃ se viyāṇāi | bhāsiyaṃ taṇubhāsae 
evam annāṇiyā nāṇaṃ | vayantā vi sayaṃ sayaṃ /
nicchayatthaṃ na jāṇanti | milakkhu vva abohiyā 
annāṇiyāṇaṃ vīmaṃsā | annāṇe na niyacchai /
appaṇo ya paraṃ nālaṃ | kuto annāṇusāsiuṃ 
vaṇe mūḍhe jahā jantū | mūḍhe neyāṇugāmie /
do vi ee akoviyā | tivvaṃ soyaṃ niyacchaī 
andho andhaṃ pahaṃ nento | dūram addhāṇa gacchai /
āvajje uppahaṃ jantū | adu vā panthāṇugāmie 
evam ege niyāgaṭhī | dhammam ārāhagā vayaṃ /
adu vā ahammam āvajje | na te savvajjuyaṃ vae 
evam ege viyakkāhiṃ | no annaṃ pajjuvāsiyā /
appaṇo ya viyakkāhiṃ | ayamañjū hi dummaī 
evaṃ takkāi sāhentā | dhammādhamme akoviyā /
dukkhaṃ te nāituenti | sauṇī pañjaraṃ jahā 
sayaṃ sayaṃ pasaṃsantā | garahantā paraṃ vayaṃ /
je u tattha viussanti | saṃsāraṃ te viussiyā 
ahāvaraṃ purakkhāyaṃ | kiriyāvāidarisaṇaṃ /
kammacintāpaṇaṭhāṇaṃ | saṃsārassa pavaḍḍhaṇaṃ 
jāṇaṃ kāeṇaṇāuī | avuho jaṃ ca hiṃsai /
puṭho saṃveyai paraṃ | aviyattaṃ khu sāvajjaṃ 
santime tau āyāṇā | jehiṃ kīrai pāvagaṃ /
abhikammā ya pesā ya | maṇasā aṇujāṇiyā 
ee u tau āyāṇā | jehiṃ kīrai pāvagaṃ /
evaṃ bhāvavisohīe | nivvāṇam abhigacchaī 
puttaṃ piyā samārabbha | āhārejja asaṃjae /
bhuñjamāṇo ya mehāvī | kammuṇā novalippaī 
maṇasā je padussanti | cittaṃ tesiṃ na vijjai /
aṇavajjam atahaṃ tesiṃ | na te saṃvuḍacāriṇo 
icceyāhi ya diṭhīhiṃ | sāyāgāravanissiyā /
saraṇaṃ ti mannamāṇā | sevantī pāvagaṃ jaṇā 
jahā assāviṇiṃ nāvaṃ | jāiandho durūhiyā /
icchaī pāram āgantuṃ | antarā ya visīyaī 
evaṃ tu samaṇā ege | micchadiṭhī aṇāriyā /
saṃsārapārakaṃkhī te | saṃsāraṃ aṇupariyaanti  tti bemi ||
    || samayajjhayaṇe biiyuddeso 

 S/1.3 samayajjhayaṇe paḍhame / 
 
jaṃ kiṃci u pūikaḍaṃ | saḍḍhīmāgantumīhiyaṃ /
sahassantariyaṃ bhuñje | dupakkhaṃ ceva sevaī 
tam eva aviyāṇantā | visamaṃsi akoviyā /
macchā vesāliyā ceva | udagassabhiyāgame 
udagassa pahāveṇaṃ | sukkaṃ sigghaṃ tam enti u /
ḍhaṅkehi ya kaṅkehi ya | āmisatthehi te duhī 
evaṃ tu samaṇā ege | vaamāṇasuhesiṇo /
macchā vesāliyā ceva | ghāyam essanti -antaso 
iṇam annaṃ tu annāṇaṃ | ihamegesimāhiyaṃ /
devautte ayaṃ loe | bambhautte i āvare 
īsareṇa kaḍe loe | pahāṇāi tahāvare /
jīvājīvasamāutte | suhadukkhasamannie 
sayaṃbhuṇā kaḍe loe | ii vuttaṃ mahesiṇā /
māreṇa saṃthuyā māyā | teṇa loe asāsae 
māhaṇā samaṇā ege | āha aṇḍakaḍe jae /
aso tattam akāsī ya | ayāṇantā musaṃ vae 
saehiṃ pariyāehiṃ | logaṃ būyā kaḍe tti ya /
tattaṃ te na viyāṇanti | na viṇāsī kayāi vi 
amaṇunnasamuppāyaṃ | dukkham eva viyāṇiyā /
samuppāyam ayāṇantā | kahaṃ nāyanti saṃvaraṃ 
suddhe apāvae āyā | ihamegesimāhiyaṃ /
puṇo kiḍḍāpadoseṇaṃ | so tattha avarajjhaī 
iha saṃvuḍe muṇī jāe | pacchā hoi apāvae /
viyaḍambu jahā bhujjo | nīrayaṃ sarayaṃ tahā 
eyāṇuvīi mehāvī | bambhacereṇa te vase /
puḍho pāvāuyā savve | akkhāyāro sayaṃ sayaṃ 
sae sae uvaṭhāṇe | siddhim eva na annahā /
ahe iheva vasavattī | savvakāmasamappie 
siddhā ya te arogā ya | ihamegesimāhiyaṃ /
siddhim eva puro kāuṃ | sāsae gaḍhiyā narā 
asaṃvuḍā aṇāīyaṃ | bhamihinti puṇo puṇo /
kappakālam uvajjanti | ṭhāṇā āsurakibbisiya  tti bemi ||
    || samayajjhayaṇe taiyuddeso 

 S/1.4 samayajjhayaṇe paḍhame / 

ee jiyā bho na saraṇaṃ | bālā paṇḍiyamāṇiṇo /
hiccā -aṃ puvvasaṃjoyaṃ | siyā kiccovaesagā 
taṃ ca bhikkhū parinnāya | viyaṃ tesu na mucchae /
aṇukkasse appalīṇe | majjheṇa muṇi jāvae 
sapariggahā ya sārambhā | ihamegesimāhiyaṃ /
apariggahā aṇārambhā | bhikkhū tāṇaṃ parivvae 
kaḍesu ghāsam esejjā | viū dattesaṇaṃ care /
agiddho vippamukko ya | omāṇaṃ parivajjae 
logavāyaṃ nisāmejjā | ihamegesimāhiyaṃ /
vivarīyapannasaṃbhūyaṃ | annauttaṃ tayāṇuyaṃ 
aṇante niie loe | sāsae na viṇassaī /
antavaṃ niie loe | ii dhīro tipāsaī 
aparimāṇaṃ viyāṇāi | ihamegesimāhiyaṃ /
savvattha saparimāṇaṃ | ii dhīro tipāsaī 
je kei tasā pāṇā | ciṭhanti adu thāvarā /
pariyāe atthi se añjū | jeṇa te tasathāvarā 
urālaṃ jagao jogaṃ | vivajjāsaṃ palenti ya /
savve akkantadukkhā ya | ao savve ahiṃsiyā 
eyaṃ khu nāṇiṇo sāraṃ | jaṃ na hiṃsai kiṃcaṇa /
ahiṃsāsamayaṃ ceva | eyāvantaṃ viyāṇiyā 
vusie ya vigayagehī | āyāṇaṃ samma rakkhae /
cariyāsaṇasejjāsu | bhattapāṇe ya antaso 
eehiṃ tihi ṭhāṇehiṃ | saṃjae sayayaṃ muṇī /
ukkasaṃ jalaṇaṃ nūmaṃ | majjhatthaṃ ca vigiñcae 
samie u sayā sāhū | pañcasaṃvarasaṃvuḍe /
siehi asie bhikkhū | āmokkhāe parivvaejjāsi  tti bemi ||
     || samayajjhayaṇaṃ paḍhamaṃ 

 S/2.1 veyāliyajjhayaṇe biie /
 
saṃbujjhaha kiṃ na bujjhaha | saṃbohī khalu pecca dullahā /
no hūvaṇamanti rāiyo | no sulabhaṃ puṇarāvi jīviyaṃ 
ḍaharā buḍḍhā ya pāsaha | gabbhatthā vi cayanti māṇavā /
seṇe jaha vaayaṃ hare | evaṃ āukhayammi tuaī 
māyāhi piyāhi luppaī | no sulahā sugaī ya peccao /
eyāi bhayāi pehiyā | ārambhā viramejja suvvae 
jam iṇaṃ jagaī puḍho jagā | kammehiṃ luppanti pāṇiṇo /
sayam eva kaḍehi gāhaī | no tassa muccejjapuṭhayaṃ 
devā gandhavvarakkhasā | asurā bhūmicarā sarīsivā /
rāyā naraseṭhimāhaṇā | ṭhāṇā te vi cayanti dukkhiyā 
kāmehi ya saṃthavehi gidhdā | kammasahā kāleṇa jantavo /
tāle jaha bandhaṇaccue | evaṃ āyukhayammi tuaī 
je yāvi bahussue siyā | dhammiya māhaṇa bhikkhue siyā /
abhinūmakaḍehi mucchie | tivvaṃ te kammehi kiccaī 
aha pāsa vivegamuṭhie | avitiṇṇe iha bhāsaī dhuvaṃ /
nāhisi āraṃ kao paraṃ | vehāse kammehi kiccaī 
jai vi ya nagiṇe kise care | jai vi ya bhuñjiya māsamantaso /
je iha māyāhi mijjaī | āgantā gabbhāya -antaso 
purisorama pāvakammuṇā | paliyantaṃ maṇuyāṇa jīviyaṃ /
sannā iha kāmamucchiyā | mohaṃ janti narā asaṃvuḍā 
jayayaṃ viharāhi jogavaṃ | aṇupāṇā panthā duruttarā /
aṇusāsaṇameva pakkame | vīrehiṃ sammaṃ paveiyaṃ 
virayā vīrā samuṭhiyā | kohakāyariyāipīsaṇā /
pāṇe na haṇanti savvaso | pāvāo virayābhinivvuḍā 
na vi tā aham eva luppae | luppantī logaṃsi pāṇiṇo /
evaṃ sahiehi pāsae | anihe se puṭhe hiyāsae 
dhuṇiyā kuliyaṃ va levavaṃ | kisae deham aṇāsaṇā iha /
avihiṃsāmeva pavvae | aṇudhammo muṇiṇā paveiyo 
sauṇī jaha paṃsuguṇḍiyā | vihuṇiya dhaṃsayaī siyaṃ rayaṃ /
evaṃ daviovahāṇavaṃ | kammaṃ khavai tavassi māhaṇe 
uṭhiyam aṇagāram esaṇaṃ | samaṇaṃ ṭhāṇaṭhiyaṃ tavassiṇaṃ /
ḍaharā vuḍḍhā ya patthae | avi susso na ya taṃ labhejja no 
jai kāluṇiyāṇi kāsiyā | jai royanti ya puttakāraṇā /
daviyaṃ bhikkhū samuṭhiyaṃ | no labbhanti na saṃṭhavittave 
jai vi ya kāmehi lāviyā | jai nejjāhi -a bandhiuṃ gharaṃ /
jai jīviya nāvakaṅkhae | no labbhanti na saṃṭhavittae 
sehanti ya -aṃ mamāiṇo | māya piyā ya suyā ya bhāriyā /
posāhi -a pāsao tumaṃ | loga paraṃ pi jahāsi posaṇo 
anne annehi mucchiyā | mohaṃ janti narā asaṃvuḍā /
visamaṃ visamehi gāhiyā | te pāvehi puṇo pagabbhiyā 
tamhā davi ikkha paṇḍie | pāvāo virae bhinivvuḍe /
paṇae vīraṃ mahāvihiṃ | siddhipahaṃ neyāuyaṃ dhuvaṃ 
veyāliyamaggamāgao | maṇavayasā kāeṇa nivvuḍo /
ciccā vittaṃ ca nāyao | ārambhaṃ ca susaṃvuḍaṃ care  tti bemi ||
    || veyāliyajjhayaṇe paḍhamuddeso 

 S/2.2 veyāliyajjhayaṇe biie /
 
taya saṃ va jahāi se rayaṃ | ii saṃkhāya muṇī na majjaī /
goyannatareṇa māhaṇe | ahaseyakarī annesi iṃkhiṇī 
jo paribhavaī paraṃ jaṇaṃ | saṃsāre parivattaī mahaṃ /
adu iṃkhiṇiyā u pāviyā | ii saṃkhāya muṇī na majjaī 
je yāvi aṇāyage siyā | je vi ya pesagapesage siyā /
je moṇapayaṃ uvaṭhie | no lajje samayaṃ sayā care 
sama annayarammi saṃjame | saṃsuddhe samaṇe parivvae /
je āvakahā samāhie | davie kālam akāsi paṇḍie 
dūraṃ aṇupassiyā muṇī | tīyaṃ dhammam aṇāgayaṃ tahā /
puṭhe pharusehi māhaṇe | avi haṇṇū samayammi rīyai 
pannasamatte sayā jae | samatādhammam udāhare muṇī /
suhume u sayā alūsae | no kujjhe no māṇi māhaṇe 
bahujaṇanamaṇammi saṃvuḍo | savvaṭhehi nare aṇissie /
harae va sayā aṇāvile | dhammaṃ pādurakāsi kāsavaṃ 
bahave pāṇā puḍho siyā | patteyaṃ samayaṃ samīhiyā /
je moṇapayaṃ uvaṭhie | viraiṃ tattha akāsi paṇḍie 
dhammassa ya pārage muṇī | ārambhassa ya antae ṭhie /
soyanti ya -aṃ mamāiṇo | no labbhanti niyaṃ pariggahaṃ 
ihaloga duhāvahaṃ viū | paraloge ya duhaṃ duhāvahaṃ /
viddhaṃsaṇadhammameva taṃ | ii vijjaṃ ko gāramāvase 
mahayaṃ paligova jāṇiyā | jā vi ya vaṃdaṇapūyaṇā ihaṃ /
suhume salle duruddhare | viumaṃtā payahijja saṃthavaṃ 
ega cara ṭhāṇamāsaṇe | sayaṇe ega samāhie siyā /
bhikkhū uvahāṇavīrie | vaigutte ajjhattasaṃvuḍo 
no pīhe na yāvapaṃguṇe | dāraṃ sunnagharassa saṃjae /
puṭhe na udāhare vayaṃ | na samucche no saṃthare taṇaṃ 
jatthatthamie aṇāule | samavisamāi muṇī hiyāsae /
caragā adu vā vi bheravā | adu vā tattha sarīsivā siyā 
tiriyā maṇuyā ya divvagā | uvasaggā tivihā hiyāsiyā /
lomādīyaṃ na hārise | sunnāgāragao mahāmuṇī 
no abhikaṃkhejja jīviyaṃ | no vi ya pūyaṇapatthaṇe siyā /
abbhattham uventi bheravā | sunnāgāragayassa bhikkhuṇo 
uvaṇīyatarassa tāiṇo | bhayamāṇassa vivikkam āsaṇaṃ /
sāmāiyam āhu tassa jaṃ | jo appāṇa bhae na daṃsae 
usiṇodagatattabhoiṇo | dhammaṭhiyassa muṇissa hīmato /
saṃsagge asāhu rāihiṃ | asamāhī u tahāgayassa vi 
ahigaraṇakaḍassa bhikkhuṇo | vayamāṇassa pasajjha dāruṇaṃ /
aṭhe parihāyaī bahū | ahigaraṇaṃ na karejja paṇḍie  
sīodaga paḍi duguṃchiṇo | apaḍinnassa lavāvasappiṇo /
sāmāiyam āhu tassa jaṃ | jo gihimatte 'saṇaṃ na bhuñjaī

bāle pāvehi mijjaī | ii saṃkhāya muṇī na majjaī 
chaṃdeṇa pale imā payā | bahumāyā moheṇa pāvuḍā /

kujae aparājie jahā | akkhehiṃ kusalehi dīvayaṃ /
kaḍameva gahāya no kaliṃ | no tīyaṃ no ceva dāvaraṃ 
evaṃ logammi tāiṇā | buie je dhamme aṇuttare /
taṃ giṇha hiyaṃ ti uttamaṃ | kaḍamiva sesa vahāya paṇḍie 
uttara maṇuyāṇa ahiyā | gāmadhamma ii me aṇussuyaṃ /

mā peha purā paṇāmae | abhikaṃkhe uvahiṃ dhuṇittae /
ja dūmaṇa tehi no nayā | te jāṇanti samāhimāhiyaṃ 
no kāhie hojja saṃjae | pāsaṇie na ya saṃpasārae /
naccā dhammaṃ aṇuttaraṃ | kayakirie na yāvi māmae 
channaṃ ca pasaṃsa no kare | na ya ukkosa pagāsa māhaṇe /
tesiṃ suvivegamāhie | paṇayā jehi sujosiyaṃ dhuvaṃ 
anihe sahie susaṃvuḍe | dhammaṭhī uvahāṇavīrie /
viharejja samāhiiṃdie | attahiyaṃ khu duheṇa labbhai 
na hi nūṇa purā aṇussuyaṃ | adu vā taṃ taha no samuṭhiyaṃ /
muṇiṇā sāmāi āhiyaṃ | nāeṇaṃ jagasavvadaṃsiṇā 
evaṃ mattā mahantaraṃ | dhammam iṇaṃ sahiyā bahū jaṇā /
guruṇo chaṃdāṇuvattagā | virayā tiṇṇa mahoghamāhiyaṃ  ti bemi ||

 S/2.3 veyāliyajjhayaṇe biie / 
 
saṃvuḍakammassa bhikkhuṇo | jaṃ dukkhaṃ puṭhaṃ abohie /
taṃ saṃjamao 'vacijjaī | maraṇaṃ hecca vayanti paṇḍiyā 
je vinnavaṇāhijosiyā | saṃtiṇṇehi samaṃ viyāhiyā /
tamhā uḍḍhaṃ ti pāsahā | adakkhu kāmāiṃ rogavaṃ 
aggaṃ vaṇiehi āhiyaṃ | dhārentī rāiṇiyā ihaṃ /
evaṃ paramā mahavvayā | akkhāyā u sarāibhoyaṇā 
je iha sāyāṇugā narā | ajjhovavannā kāmehi mucchiyā /
kivaṇeṇa samaṃ pagabbhiyā | na vi jāṇanti samāhimāhiyaṃ 
vāheṇa jahā va vicchae | abale hoi gavaṃ pacoie /
se antaso appathāmae | nāivahe abale visīyai 
evaṃ kāmesaṇa viū | ajja sue payahejja saṃthavaṃ /
kāmī kāme na kāmae | laddhe vā vi aladdha kaṇhuī 
mā paccha asādhutā bhave | accehī aṇusāsa appagaṃ /
ahiyaṃ ca asāhu soyaī | se thaṇaī paridevaī bahuṃ 
iha jīviyam eva pāsahā | taruṇe vā sasayassa tuaī /
ittaravāse ya bujjhaha | giddha narā kāmesu mucchiyā 
je iha ārambhanissiyā | āyadaṇḍa egantalūsagā /
gantā te pāvalogayaṃ | cirarāyaṃ āsuriyaṃ disaṃ 
na ya saṃkhayam āhu jīviyaṃ | taha vi ya bālajaṇo pagabbhaī /
paccuppanneṇa kāriyaṃ | ko daṭhuṃ paralogam āgae 
adakkhuva dakkhuvāhiyaṃ | taṃ saddahasu adakkhudaṃsaṇā /
haṃdi hu suniruddhadaṃsaṇe | mohaṇieṇa kaḍeṇa kammuṇā 
dukkhī mohe puṇo puṇo | nivvindejja silogapūyaṇaṃ /
evaṃ sahie hipāsae | āyatulaṃ pāṇehi saṃjae 
gāraṃ pi ya āvase nare | aṇupuvvaṃ pāṇehi saṃjae /
samatā savvattha suvvae | devāṇaṃ gacche salogayaṃ 
soccā bhagavāṇusāsaṇaṃ | sacce tattha karejjuvakkamaṃ /
savvattha viṇīyamacchare | uñchaṃ bhikkhu visuddhamāhare 
evaṃ naccā ahiṭhae | dhammaṭhī uvahāṇavīrie /
gutte jutte sayā jae | āyapare paramāyataṭhie 
vittaṃ pasavo ya nāio | taṃ bāle saraṇaṃ ti mannaī /
ee mama tesu vī ahaṃ | no tāṇaṃ saraṇaṃ na vijjaī 
abbhāgamiyammi vā duhe | ahavā ukkamie bhavantie /
egassa gaī ya āgaī | vidumantā saraṇaṃ na mannaī 
savve sayakammakappiyā | aviyatteṇa duheṇa pāṇiṇo /
hiṇḍanti bhayāulā saḍhā | jāijarāmaraṇehi bhidduyā 
iṇameva khaṇaṃ viyāṇiyā | no sulabhaṃ bohiṃ ca āhiyaṃ /
evaṃ sahie hipāsae | āha jiṇe iṇam eva sesagā 
abhaviṃsu purā vi bhikkhuvo | āesā vi bhavanti suvvayā /
eyāiṃ guṇāiṃ āhu te | kāsavassa aṇudhammacāriṇo 
tiviheṇa vi pāṇa mā haṇe | āyahie aṇiyāṇa saṃvuḍe /
evaṃ siddhā aṇantaso | saṃpai je ya aṇāgayāvare 
(evaṃ se udāhu aṇuttaranāṇī | aṇuttaradaṃsī aṇuttaranāṇadaṃsaṇadhare /)
(arahā nāyaputte | bhagavaṃ vesālie viyāhie  tti bemi ||)

S/3.1 uvasaggajjhayaṇe taie / 

sūraṃ mannai appāṇaṃ | jāva jeyaṃ na passaī /
jujjhantaṃ daḍhadhammāṇaṃ | sisupālo va mahārahaṃ 
payāyā sūrā raṇasīse | saṃgāmammi uvaṭhie /
māyā puttaṃ na jāṇāi | jeeṇa parivicchae 
evaṃ sehe vi appuṭhe | bhikkhāyariyāakovie /
sūraṃ mannai appāṇaṃ | jāva lūhaṃ na sevae 
jayā hemantamāsammi | sīyaṃ phusai savvagaṃ /
tattha mandā visīyanti | rajjahīṇā va khattiyā 
phuṭhe gimhāhitāvenaṃ | vimaṇe supivāsie /
tattha mandā visīyanti | macchā appodae jahā 
sayā dattesaṇā dukkhā | jāyaṇā duppaṇolliyā /
kammattā dubbhagā ceva | iccāhaṃsu puḍhojaṇā 
ee sadde acāyantā | gāmesu nagaresu vā /
tattha mandā visīyanti | saṃgāmammi va bhīruyā 
appege khudhiyaṃ bhikkhuṃ | suṇī ḍaṃsai lūsae /
tattha mandā visīyanti | teupuṭhā va pāṇiṇo 
appege paḍibhāsanti | paḍipanthiyam āgayā /
paḍiyāragayā ee | je ee evajīviṇo 
appege vai juñjanti | nagiṇā piṇḍolagāhamā /
muṇḍā kaṇḍūviṇaṭhaṅgā | ujjalā asamāhiyā 
evaṃ vippaḍivannege | appaṇā u ajāṇayā /
tamāo te tamaṃ janti | mandā moheṇa pāvuḍā 
puṭho ya daṃsamasagehiṃ | taṇaphāsamacāiyā /
na me diṭhe pare loe | jai paraṃ maraṇaṃ siyā 
saṃtattā kesaloeṇaṃ | bambhaceraparāiyā /
tattha mandā visīyanti | macchā viṭhā va keyaṇe 
āyadaṇḍasamāyāre | micchāsaṃṭhiyabhāvaṇā /
harisappaosamāvannā | keī lūsantināriyā 
appege paliyantesiṃ | cāro coro tti suvvayaṃ /
bandhanti bhikkhuyaṃ bālā | kasāyavayaṇehi ya 
tattha daṇḍeṇa saṃvīte | muṭhiṇā adu phaleṇa vā /
nāīṇaṃ saraī bāle | itthī vā kuddhagāmiṇī 
ee bho kasiṇā phāsā | pharusā durahiyāsayā /
hatthī vā sarasaṃvittā | kīvāvasa gayā gihaṃ  ti bemi ||
     || uvasaggajjhayaṇe paḍhamuddese 

 S/3.2 uvasaggajjhayaṇe taie /
 
ahime suhumā saṃgā | bhikkhūṇaṃ je duruttarā /
jattha ege visīyanti | na cayanti javittae 
appege nāyagā dissa | royanti parivāriyā /
posa -e tāya puṭho si | kassa tāya jahāsi -e 
piyā te therao tāya | sasā te khuḍḍiyā imā /
bhāyaro te sagā tāya | soyarā kiṃ jahāsi -e 
māyaraṃ piyaraṃ posa | evaṃ logo bhavissai /
evaṃ khu loiyaṃ tāya | je pālenti ya māyaraṃ 
uttarā mahurullāvā | puttā te tāya khuḍḍayā /
bhāriyā te navā tāya | mā sā annaṃ jaṇaṃ game 
ehi tāya gharaṃ jāmo | mā ya kamme sahā vayaṃ /
biiyaṃ pi tāya pāsāmo | jāmu tāva sayaṃ gihaṃ 
gantuṃ tāya puṇo gacche | na teṇāsamaṇo siyā /
akāmagaṃ parikkammaṃ | ko te vārium arihai 
jaṃ kiṃci aṇagaṃ tāya | taṃ pi savvaṃ samīkayaṃ /
hiraṇṇaṃ vavahārāi | taṃ pi dāhāmu te vayaṃ 
icceva -aṃ susehanti | kāluṇīyasamuṭhiyā /
vibaddho nāisaṃgehiṃ | tao gāraṃ pahāvai 
jahā rukkhaṃ vaṇe jāyaṃ | māluyā paḍibandhai /
evaṃ -aṃ paḍibandhanti | nāio asamāhiṇā 
vibaddho nāisaṃgehiṃ | hatthī vā vi navaggahe /
piṭhao parisappanti | suya go vva adūrae 
ee saṃgā maṇūsāṇaṃ | pāyālā va atārimā /
kīvā jattha ya kissanti | nāisaṃgehi mucchiyā 
taṃ ca bhikkhū parinnāya | savve saṃgā mahāsavā /
jīviyaṃ nāyakaṃkhijjā | soccā dhammam aṇuttaraṃ 
ahime santi āvaā | kāsaveṇaṃ paveiyā /
buddhā jatthāvasappanti | sīyanti abuhā jahiṃ 
rāyāṇo rāyamaccā ya | māhaṇā adu va khattiyā /
nimantayanti bhogehiṃ | bhikkhuyaṃ sāhujīviṇaṃ 
hatthassarahajāṇehiṃ | vihāragamaṇehi ya /
bhuñja bhoge ime sagghe | maharisī pūjayāmu taṃ 
vatthagandham alaṃkāraṃ | itthīo sayaṇāṇi ya /
bhuñjāhimāiṃ bhogāiṃ | āuso pūjayāmu taṃ 
jo tume niyamo ciṇṇo | bhikkhubhāvammi suvvayā /
agāram āvasantassa | savvo saṃvijjae tahā 
ciraṃ dūijjamāṇassa | doso dāṇiṃ kuo tava /
icceva -aṃ nimantenti | nīvāreṇa va sūyaraṃ 
coiyā bhikkhacariyāe | acayantā javittae /
tattha mandā visīyanti | ujjāṇaṃsi va dubbalā 
acayantā va lūheṇaṃ | uvahāṇeṇa tajjiyā /
tattha mandā visīyanti | ujjāṇaṃsi jaraggavā 
evaṃ nimantaṇaṃ laddhuṃ | mucchiyā giddha itthisu /
ajjhovavannā kāmehiṃ | noijjantā gayā gihaṃ  ti bemi ||
     || uvasaggajjhayaṇe biiyuddese 

 S/3.3 uvasaggajjhayaṇe taie /
 
jahā saṃgāmakālammi | piṭhao bhīru vehai /
valayaṃ gahaṇaṃ nūmaṃ | ko jāṇai parājayaṃ 
muhuttāṇaṃ muhuttassa | muhutto hoi tāriso /
parājiyā vasappāmo | ii bhīrū uvehaī 
evaṃ u samaṇā ege | abalaṃ naccāṇa appagaṃ /
aṇāgayaṃ bhayaṃ dissa | avakappantimaṃ suyaṃ 
ko jāṇai viūvāyaṃ | itthīo udagāu vā /
coijjantā pavakkhāmo | na no atthi pakappiyaṃ 
icceva paḍilehanti | valayā paḍilehiṇo /
vitigicchasamāvannā | panthāṇaṃ ca akoviyā 
je u saṃgāmakālammi | nāyā sūrapuraṃgamā /
no te piṭham uvehinti | kiṃ paraṃ maraṇaṃ siyā 
evaṃ samuṭhie bhikkhū | vosijjā gārabandhaṇaṃ /
ārambhaṃ tiriyaṃ kau | attattāe parivvae

saṃbaddhasamakappā u | annamannesu mucchiyā /

naṭhasappahasabbhāvā | saṃsārassa apāragā 
aha te paribhāsejjā | bhikkhu mokkhavisārae /
evaṃ tubbhe pabhāsantā | dupakkhaṃ ceva sevaha

taṃ ca bīodagaṃ bhoccā | tam uddissādi jaṃ kaḍaṃ 
littā tivvābhitāveṇaṃ | ujjhiyā asamāhiyā /
nāikaṇḍūiyaṃ seyaṃ | aruyassāvarajjhaī 
tatteṇa aṇusiṭhā te | apaḍinneṇa jāṇayā /
na esa niyae magge | asamikkhā vaī kiī 
erisā jā vaī esā | aggaveṇu vva karisiyā /
gihiṇo abhihaḍaṃ seyaṃ | bhuñjiuṃ na u bhikkhuṇaṃ 
dhammapannavaṇā jā sā | sārambhā na visohiyā /
na u eyāhi diṭhīhiṃ | puvvam āsiṃ pagappiyaṃ 
savvāhiṃ aṇujuttīhiṃ | acayantā javittae /
tao vāyaṃ nirākiccā | te bhujjo vi pagabbhiyā 
rāgadosāmibhūyappā | micchatteṇa abhidduyā /
āusse saraṇaṃ janti | aṃkaṇā iva pavvayaṃ 
bahuguṇappagappāiṃ | kujjā attasamāhie /
jeṇanne na virujjhejjā | teṇa taṃ taṃ samāyare 
imaṃ ca dhammam āyāya | kāsaveṇa paveiyaṃ /
kujjā bhikkhū gilāṇassa | agilāe samāhie 
saṃkhāya pesalaṃ dhammaṃ | diṭhimaṃ parinivvuḍe /
uvasagge niyāmittā | āmokkhāe parivvaejjāsi  tti bemi ||

 S/3.4 uvasaggajjhayaṇe taie /

S/4.1 itthiparinnajjhayaṇe cautthe / 
 
je māyaraṃ ca piyaraṃ ca | vippajahāya puvvasaṃjogaṃ /
ege sahie carissāmi | ārayamehuṇo vivittesu 
suhumeṇaṃ taṃ parikkamma | channapaeṇa itthio mandā /
uvvāyaṃ pi tāu jāṇaṃsu | jahā lissanti bhikkhuṇo ege 
pāse bhisaṃ nisīyanti | abhikkhaṇaṃ posavatthaṃ parihinti /
kāyaṃ ahe vi daṃsanti | bāhū uddhau kakkhamaṇuvvae 
sayaṇāsaṇehi jogehiṃ | itthiyo egayā nimantenti /
eyāṇi ceva se jāṇe | pāsāṇi virūvarūvāṇi 
no tāsu cakkhu saṃdhejjā | no vi ya sāhasaṃ samabhijāṇe /
no sahiyaṃ pi viharejjā | evam appā surakkhio hoi 
āmantiya ussaviyā | bhikkhuṃ āyasā nimantenti /
eyāṇi ceva se jāṇe | saddāṇi virūvarūvāṇi 
maṇabandhaṇehi -egehiṃ | kaluṇaviṇīyam uvagasittāṇaṃ /
adu mañjulāiṃ bhāsanti | āṇavayanti bhinnakahāhiṃ 
sīhaṃ jahā va kuṇimeṇaṃ | nibbhayam egacaraṃ ti pāseṇaṃ /
evitthiyāu bandhanti | saṃvuḍaṃ egaiyam aṇagāraṃ 
aha tattha puṇo namayantī | rahakāro va nemi āṇupuvvīe /
baddho mie va pāseṇaṃ | phandante vi na muccae tāhe 
aha se 'utappaī pacchā | bhoccā pāyasaṃ va visamissaṃ /
evaṃ vivegam āyāya | saṃvāso na vi kappae davie 
tamhā u vajjae itthī | visalittaṃ va kaṇagaṃ naccā /
oe kulāṇi basavattī | āghāe na se vi nigganthe 
je eyaṃ uñchaṃ aṇugiddhā | annayarā honti kusīlāṇaṃ /
sutavassie vi se bhikkhū | no vihare saha -am itthīsu 
avi dhūyarāhi suṇhāhiṃ | dhāīhiṃ aduva dāsīhiṃ /
mahaīhi vā kumārīhiṃ | saṃthavaṃ se na kujjā aṇagāre 
adu nāiṇaṃ ca suhīṇaṃ vā | appiyaṃ daṭhu egayā hoi /
giddhā sattā kāmehiṃ | rakkhaṇaposaṇe maṇusso 'si 
samaṇaṃ pi daṭhudāsīṇaṃ | tattha vi tāva ege kuppanti /
adu vā bhoyaṇehi natthehiṃ | itthīdosaṃ saṃkiṇo honti 
kuvvanti saṃthavaṃ tāhiṃ | pabbhaṭhā samāhijogehiṃ /
tamhā samaṇā na samenti | āyahiyāe saṃnisejjāo 
bahave gihāiṃ avahau | missībhāvaṃ patthuyā ya ege /
dhuvamaggam eva pavayanti | vāyāvīriyaṃ kusīlāṇaṃ 
suddhaṃ ravai parisāe | aha rahassammi dukkaḍaṃ karenti /
jāṇanti ya -aṃ tahāviū | māille mahāsaḍhe 'yaṃ ti 
sayaṃ dukkaḍaṃ ca na vayai | āiṭho vi pakatthai bāle /
veyāṇuvīi mā kāsī | coijjanto gilāi se bhuñjo 
osiyā vi itthiposesu | purisā itthiveyakheyannā /
pannāsamanniyā vege | nārīṇaṃ vasaṃ uvakasanti 
avi hatthapāyacheyāe | adu vā vaddhamaṃsaukkante /
avi teyasābhitāvaṇāṇi | tacchiya khārasiṃcaṇāiṃ ya 
adu kaṇṇanāsacheyaṃ | kaṇṭhaccheyaṇaṃ tiikkhantī /
ii ettha pāvasaṃtattā | na venti puṇo na kāhinti 
suyam eyam evam egesiṃ | itthīveya tti hu suyakkhāyaṃ /
eyaṃ pi tā vaittāṇaṃ | adu vā kammuṇā avakarenti 
annaṃ maṇeṇa cintenti | vāyā annaṃ ca kammuṇā annaṃ /

juvaī samaṇaṃ būyā | vicittalaṃkāravatthagāṇi parihittā /
virayā carissahaṃ rukkhaṃ | dhammam āikkha -e bhayantāro

jaukumbhe jahā uvajjoī | saṃvāse viū visīejjā

evitthiyāhi aṇagārā | saṃvāseṇa nāsam uvayanti 
kuvvanti pāvagaṃ kammaṃ | puṭhā vegevam āhiṃsu /
no haṃ karemi pāvaṃ ti | aṃkesāiṇī mamesa tti 
bālassa mandayaṃ bīyaṃ | jaṃ ca kaḍaṃ avajāṇai bhujjo /
duguṇaṃ karei se pāvaṃ | pūyaṇakāmo visannesī 
saṃlokaṇijjam aṇagāraṃ | āyagayaṃ nimantaṇeṇāhaṃsu /

 S/4.2 itthiparinnajjhayaṇe cautthe /
 
oe sayā na rajjejjā | bhogakāmī puṇo virajjejjā /
bhoge samaṇāṇa suṇeha | jaha bhuñjanti bhikkhuṇo ege 
aha taṃ tu bheyam āvannaṃ | mucchiyaṃ bhikkhuṃ kāmamaivaaṃ /
palibhindiyā -aṃ to pacchā | pāduddhau muddhi pahaṇanti 
jai kesiyā -aṃ mae bhikkhu | no vihare saha -am itthīe /
kesāṇavi haṃ luñcissaṃ | nannattha mae carejjāsi 
aha -aṃ se hoi uvaladdho | to pesanti tahābhūehiṃ /
alāuccheyaṃ pehehi | vagguphalāiṃ āharāhi tti 
dārūṇi sāgapāgāe | pajjoo vā bhavissaī rāo /
pāyāṇi ya me rayāvehi | ehi tā me piṭhaomadde 
vatthāṇi ya me paḍilehehi | annaṃ pāṇaṃ ca āharāhi tti /
gandhaṃ ca raoharaṇaṃ ca | kāsavagaṃ ca me samaṇujāṇāhi 
adu añjaṇiṃ alaṃkāraṃ | kukkayayaṃ me payacchāhi /
loddhaṃ ca loddhakusumaṃ ca | veṇupalāsiyaṃ ca guliyaṃ ca 
kuṭhaṃ tagaraṃ ca agaruṃ | saṃpiṭhaṃ sammaṃ usireṇaṃ /
tellaṃ muhamiñjāe | veṇuphalāiṃ saṃnihāṇāe 
nandīcuṇṇagāiṃ pāharāhi | chattovāṇahaṃ ca jāṇāhi /
satthaṃ ca sūvacchejjāe | āṇīlaṃ ca vatthayaṃ rayāvehi 
suphaṇiṃ ca sāgapāgāe | āmalagāiṃ dagāharaṇaṃ ca /
tilagakaraṇim añjaṇasalāgaṃ | ghiṃsu me vihūṇayaṃ vijāṇehi 
saṃḍāsagaṃ ca phaṇihaṃ ca | sīhalipāsagaṃ ca āṇāhi /
ādaṃsagaṃ ca payacchāhi | dantapakkhālaṇaṃ pavesāhi 
pūgaphalaṃ taṃbollayaṃ | sūi suttagaṃ ca jāṇāhi /
kosaṃ ca moyamehāe | suppukkhalagaṃ ca khāragālaṇaṃ ca 
candālagaṃ ca karagaṃ ca | vaccagharaṃ ca āuso khaṇāhi /
sarapāyayaṃ ca jāyāe | gorahagaṃ ca sāmaṇerāe 
ghaḍigaṃ ca saḍiṇḍimayaṃ ca | celagolaṃ kumārabhūyāe /
vāsaṃ samabhiāvaṇṇaṃ | āvasahaṃ ca jāṇa bhattaṃ ca 
āsandiyaṃ ca navasuttaṃ | pāullāiṃ saṃkamaṭhāe /
adu puttadohalaṭhāe | āṇappā havanti dāsā vā 
jāe phale samuppanne | geṇhasu vā -aṃ ahavā jahāhi /
aha puttaposiṇo ege | bhāravahā havanti uā vā 
rāo vi uṭhiyā santā | dāragaṃ ca saṃṭhavanti dhāī vā /
suhirāmaṇā vi te santā | vatthadhovā havanti haṃsā vā 
evaṃ bahuhiṃ kayapuvvaṃ | bhogatthāe je 'bhiyāvannā /
dāse mie va pese vā | pasubhūe va se na vā keī 
evaṃ khu tāsu vinnappaṃ | saṃthavaṃ saṃvāsaṃ ca vajjejjā /
tajjātiyā ime kāmā | vajjakarā ya evam akkhāe 
eyaṃ bhayaṃ na seyāe | ii se appagaṃ nirumbhittā /
no itthiṃ no pasuṃ bhikkhu | no sayaṃ pāṇiṇā nilijjejjā 
suvisuddhalese mehāvī | parakiriyaṃ ca vajjae nāṇī /
maṇasā vayasā kāeṇaṃ | savvaphāsasahe aṇagāre 
icc evam āhu se vīre | dhuyarae dhuyamohe se bhikkhu /
tamhā ajjhattavisuddhe suvimukke | āmokkhāe parivvaejjāsi  tti bemi ||
     || itthiparinnajjhayaṇaṃ cautthaṃ 

 S/5.1 nirayavibhattiyajjhayaṇe pañcame /
 
pucchissahaṃ kevaliyaṃ mahesiṃ | kahaṃ bhitāvā naragā puratthā /
ajāṇao me muṇi būhi jāṇaṃ | kahiṃ nu bālā naragaṃ uventi 
evaṃ mae puṭha mahāṇubhāve | iṇamo 'bbavī kāsave āsupanne /
paveyaissaṃ duham aṭhaduggaṃ | āīṇiyaṃ dukkaḍiṇaṃ puratthā 
je kei bālā iha jīviyaṭhī | pāvāiṃ kammāiṃ karenti ruddā /
te ghorarūve tamisandhayāre | tivvābhitāve narage paḍanti 
tivvaṃ tase pāṇiṇo thāvare ya | je hiṃsaī āyasuhaṃ paḍuccā /
je lūsae hoi adattahārī | na sikkhaī seyaviyassa kiṃci 
pāgabbhi pāṇe bahuṇaṃ tivāī | anivvue ghāyam uvei bāle /
niho nisaṃ gacchai antakāle | ahosiraṃ kau uvei duggaṃ 
haṇa chindaha bhindaha -aṃ daheti | sadde suṇentā paradhammiyāṇaṃ /
te nāragāo bhayabhinnasannā | kaṃkhanti kaṃ nāma disaṃ vayāmo 
iṅgālarāsiṃ jaliyaṃ sajoiṃ | tattovamaṃ bhūmim aṇukkamantā /
te ḍajjhamāṇā kaluṇaṃ thaṇanti | arahassarā tattha ciraṭhiīyā 
jai te suyā veyaraṇī bhiduggā | nisio jahā khura iva tikkhasoyā /
taranti te veyaraṇiṃ bhiduggaṃ | usucoiyā sattisu hammamāṇā 
kīlehi vijjhanti asāhukammā | nāvaṃ uvente saivippahūṇā /
anne u sūlāhi tisūliyāhiṃ | dīhāhi viddhūṇa ahe karenti 
kesiṃ ca bandhittu gale silāo | udagaṃsi bolenti mahālayaṃsi /
kalaṃbuyāvāluyamummure ya | lolanti paccanti ya tattha anne 
āsūriyaṃ nāma mahābhitāvaṃ | andhaṃ tamaṃ duppataraṃ mahantaṃ /
uḍḍhaṃ ahe yaṃ tiriyaṃ disāsu | samāhio jatthagaṇī jhiyāi 
jaṃsī guhāe jalaṇe 'tiue | avijāṇao ḍajjhai luttapanno /
sayā ya kaluṇaṃ puṇa ghammaṭhāṇaṃ | gāḍhovaṇīyaṃ aidukkhadhammaṃ 
cattāri agaṇīo samārabhettā | jahiṃ kūrakammā bhitaventi bālaṃ /
te tattha ciṭhantabhitappamāṇā | macchā va jīvanto va joipattā 
saṃtacchaṇaṃ nāma mahābhitāvaṃ | te nāragā jattha asāhukammā /
hatthehi pāehi ya bandhiūṇaṃ | phalagaṃ va tacchanti kuhāḍahatthā 
ruhire puṇo vaccasamussiyaṃge | bhinnattamaṃge parivattayantā /
payanti -aṃ neraie phurante | sajīvamacche va ayokavalle 
no ceva te tattha masībhavanti | na majjaī tivvabhiveyaṇāe /
tamāṇubhāgaṃ aṇuveyayantā | dukkhanti dukkhī iha dukkaḍeṇaṃ 
tahiṃ ca te lolaṇasaṃpagāḍhe | gāḍhaṃ sutattaṃ agaṇiṃ vayanti /
na tattha sāyaṃ lahaī bhidugge | arahiyābhitāvā taha vī taventi 
se succaī nagaravahe vva sadde | duhovaṇīyāṇi payāṇi tattha /
udiṇṇakammāṇa udiṇṇakammā | puṇo puṇo te sarahaṃ duhenti 
pāṇehi -aṃ pāva viyojayanti | taṃ bhe pavakkhāmi jahātaheṇaṃ /
daṇḍehi tatthā sarayanti bālā | savvehi daṇḍehi purākaehiṃ 
te hammamāṇā narage paḍanti | puṇṇe durūvassa mahābhitāve /
te tattha ciṭhanti durūvabhakkhī | tuanti kammovagayā kimīhiṃ 
sayā kasiṇaṃ puṇo ghammaṭhāṇaṃ | gāḍhovaṇīyaṃ aidukkhadhammaṃ /
andūsu pakkhippa vihattu dehaṃ | veheṇa sīsaṃ se 'bhitāvayanti 
chindanti bālassa khureṇa nakkaṃ | oṭhe vi chindanti duve vi kaṇṇe /
jibbhaṃ viṇikkassa vihatthimettaṃ | tikkhāhi sūlāhi bhitāvayanti 
te tippamāṇā talasaṃpuḍaṃ va | rāiṃdiyaṃ tattha thaṇanti bālā /
galanti te soṇiyapūyamaṃsaṃ | pajjoiyā khārapaiddhiyaṃgā 
jai te suyā lohiyapūyapāī | bālāgaṇī teaguṇā pareṇaṃ /
kumbhī mahantāhiyaporusīyā | samūsiyā lohiyapūyapuṇṇā 
pakkhippa tāsuṃ payayanti bāle | aassare te kaluṇaṃ rasante /
taṇhāiyā te tautambatattaṃ | pajjijjamāṇaayaraṃ rasanti 
appeṇa appaṃ iha vañcaittā | bhavāhame puvvasae sahasse /
ciṭhanti tatthā bahukūrakammā | jahā kaḍaṃ kamma tahāsi bhāre 
samajjiṇittā kalusaṃ aṇajjā | iṭhehi kantehi ya vippahūṇā /
te dubbhigandhe kasiṇe ya phāse | kammovagā kuṇime āvasanti  tti bemi ||

 S/5.2 nirayavibhattiyajjhayaṇe pañcame /
 
ahāvaraṃ sāsayadukkhadhammaṃ | taṃ bhe pavakkhāmi jahātaheṇaṃ /
bālā jahā dukkaḍakammakārī | veyanti kammāiṃ purekaḍāiṃ 
hatthehi pāehi ya bandhiūṇaṃ | uyaraṃ vikattanti khurāsiehiṃ /
giṇhittu bālassa vihattu dehaṃ | vaddhaṃ thiraṃ piṭhau uddharanti 
bāhū pakattanti ya mūlao se | thūlaṃ viyāsaṃ muhe āḍahanti /
rahaṃsi juttaṃ sarayanti bālaṃ | ārussa vijjhanti tudeṇa piṭhe 
ayaṃ va tattaṃ jaliyaṃ sajoi | taūvamaṃ bhūmim aṇukkamantā /
te ḍajjhamāṇā kaluṇaṃ thaṇanti | usucoiyā tattajugesu juttā 
bālā balā bhūmim aṇukkamantā | pavijjalaṃ lohapahaṃ ca tattaṃ /
jaṃsī bhiduggaṃsi pavajjamāṇā | pese va daṇḍehi purā karenti 
te saṃpagāḍhaṃsi pavajjamāṇā | silāhi hammanti nipātiṇīhiṃ /
saṃtāvaṇī nāma ciraṭhiīyā | saṃtappaī jattha asāhukammā 
kandūsu pakkhippa payanti bālaṃ | tao vi daḍḍhā puṇa uppayanti /
te uḍḍhakāehi pakhajjamāṇā | avarehi khajjanti saṇapphaehiṃ 
samūsiyaṃ nāma vidhūmaṭhāṇaṃ | jaṃ soyatattā kaluṇaṃ thaṇanti /
ahosiraṃ kau vigattiūṇaṃ | ayaṃ va satthehi samosaventi 
samūsiyā tattha visūṇiyaṃgā | pakkhīhi khajjanti ayomuhehiṃ /
saṃjīvaṇī nāma ciraṭhiīyā | jaṃsī payā hammai pāvaceyā 
tikkhāhi sūlāhi nivāyayanti | vasogayaṃ sāvayayaṃ va laddhaṃ /
te sūlaviddhā kaluṇaṃ thaṇanti | egantadukkhaṃ duhao gilāṇā 
sayā jalaṃ nāma nihaṃ mahantaṃ | jaṃsī jalanto agaṇī akaṭho /
ciṭhanti baddhā bahukūrakammā | arahassarā kei ciraṭhiīyā 
ciyā mahantīu samārabhittā | chubbhanti te ta kaluṇaṃ rasantaṃ /
āvaaī tattha asāhukammā | sappī jahā paḍiyaṃ joimajjhe 
sayā kasiṇaṃ puṇa ghammaṭhāṇaṃ | gāḍhovaṇīyaṃ aidukkhadhammaṃ /
hatthehi pāehi ya bandhiūṇaṃ | sattu vva daṇḍehi samārabhanti 
bhañjanti bālassa vaheṇa puṭhī | sīsaṃ pi bhindanti ayoghaṇehiṃ /
te bhinnadehā phalagaṃ va tacchā | tattāhi ārāhi niyojayanti 
abhijuṃjiyā rudda asāhukammā | usucoiyā hatthivahaṃ vahanti /
egaṃ durūhittu duve tao vā | ārussa vijjhanti kakāṇao se 
bālā balā bhūmim aṇukkamantā | pavijjalaṃ kaṇailaṃ mahantaṃ /
vivaddhatappehi vivaṇṇacitte | samīriyā koabaliṃ karenti 
veyālie nāma mahābhitāve | egāyae pavvayam antalikkhe /
hammanti tatthā bahukūrakammā | paraṃ sahassāṇa muhuttagāṇaṃ 
saṃbāhiyā dukkaḍiṇo thaṇanti | aho ya rāo paritappamāṇā /
egantakūḍe narage mahante | kūḍeṇa tatthā visame hayā u 
bhañjanti -aṃ puvvamarī sarosaṃ | samuggare te musale gaheuṃ /
te bhinnadehā ruhiraṃ vamantā | omuddhagā dharaṇitale paḍanti 
aṇāsiyā nāma mahāsiyālā | pāgabbhiṇo tattha sayāvakovā /
khajjanti tatthā bahukūrakammā | adūragā saṃkhaliyāhi baddhā 
sayājalā nāma naī bhiduggā | pavijjalaṃ lohavilīṇatattā /
jaṃsī bhiduggaṃsi pavajjamāṇā | egāyatāṇukkamaṇaṃ karenti 
eyāiṃ phāsāiṃ phusanti bālaṃ | nirantaraṃ tattha ciraṭhiīyaṃ /
na hammamāṇassa u hoi tāṇaṃ | ego sayaṃ paccaṇuhoi dukkhaṃ 
jaṃ jārisaṃ puvvamakāsi kammaṃ | tam eva āgacchai saṃparāe /
egantadukkhaṃ bhavamajjaṇittā | veyanti dukkhī tam aṇantadukkhaṃ 
eyāṇi soccā naragāṇi dhīre | na hiṃsae kiṃcaṇa savvaloe /
egantadiṭhī apariggahe u | bujjhijja logassa vasaṃ na gacche 
evaṃ tirikkhe maṇuyāmaresuṃ | caurantaṇantaṃ tayaṇuvvivāgaṃ /
sa savvam eyaṃ ii veyaittā | kaṃkhejja kālaṃ dhuyam āyarejja  tti bemi ||

 S/6 sirivīratthuiyajjhayaṇe chaṭhe /
 
pucchissu -aṃ samaṇā māhaṇā ya | agāriṇo yā paratitthiyā ya /
se kei negantahiyaṃ dhammam āhu | aṇelisaṃ sāhusamikkhayāe 
kahaṃ ca nāṇaṃ kaha daṃsaṇaṃ se | sīlaṃ kahaṃ nāyasuyassa āsi /
jāṇāsi -aṃ bhikkhu jahātaheṇaṃ | ahāsuyaṃ būhi jahā nisantaṃ 
kheyannae se kusalāsupanne | aṇantanāṇī ya aṇantadaṃsī /
jasaṃsiṇo cakkhupahe ṭhiyassa | jāṇāhi dhammaṃ ca dhiiṃ ca pehi 
uḍḍhaṃ ahe yaṃ tiriyaṃ disāsu | tasā ya je thāvara je ya pāṇā /
se niccaniccehi samikkha panne | dīve va dhammaṃ samiyaṃ udāhu 
se savvadaṃsī abhibhūyanāṇī | nirāmagandhe dhiimaṃ ṭhiyappā /
aṇuttare savvajagaṃsi vijjaṃ | ganthā aīe abhae aṇāū 
se bhūipanne aṇieacārī | ohaṃtare dhīre aṇantacakkhū /
aṇuttaraṃ tappai sūrie vā | vairoyaṇinde va tamaṃ pagāse 
aṇuttaraṃ dhammam iṇaṃ jiṇāṇaṃ | neyā muṇī kāsava āsupanne /
inde va devāṇa mahāṇubhāve | sahassaṇeyā divi -aṃ visiṭhe 
se pannayā akkhayasāgare vā | madodahī vā vi aṇantapāre /
aṇāvile vā akasāi mukke | sakke va devāhivaī juīmaṃ 
se vīrieṇaṃ paḍipuṇṇavīrie | sudaṃsaṇe vā nagasavvaseṭhe /
surālae vā si mudāgare se | virāyae negaguṇovavee 
sayaṃ sahassāṇa u joyaṇāṇaṃ | tikaṇḍage paṇḍagavejayante /
se joyaṇe navanavate sahasse | uddhussiyo heṭha sahassam egaṃ 
puṭhe nabhe ciṭhai bhūmivaṭhie | jaṃ sūriyā aṇuparivaayanti /
se hemavaṇṇe bahunandaṇe ya | jaṃsī raiṃ veyayaī mahindā 
se pavvae saddamahappagāse | virāyaī kañcaṇamaṭhavaṇṇe /
aṇuttare girisu ya pavvadugge | girīvare se jalie va bhome 
mahīi majjhammi ṭhie naginde | pannāyae sūriyasuddhalese /
evaṃ sirīe u sa bhūrivaṇṇe | maṇorame joyai accimālī 
sudaṃsaṇasseva jaso girissa | pavuccaī mahao pavvayassa /
eovame samaṇe nāyaputte | jāījasodaṃsaṇanāṇasīle 
girīvare vā nisahāyayāṇaṃ | ruyae va seṭhe valayāyayāṇaṃ /
taovame se jagabhūipanne | muṇīṇa majjhe tam udāhu panne 
aṇuttaraṃ dhammam udīraittā | aṇuttaraṃ jhāṇavaraṃ jhiyāi /
susukkasukkaṃ apagaṇḍasukkaṃ | saṃkhinduegantavadāyasukkaṃ 
aṇuttaraggaṃ paramaṃ mahesī | asesakammaṃ sa visohaittā /
siddhiṃ gae sāimaṇantapatte | nāṇeṇa sīleṇa ya daṃsaṇeṇa 
rukkhesu nāe jaha sāmalī vā | jassiṃ raiṃ veyayaī suvaṇṇā /
vaṇesu vā nandaṇam āhu seṭhaṃ | nāṇeṇa sīleṇa ya bhūipanne 
thaṇiyaṃ va saddāṇa aṇuttare u | cando va tārāṇa mahāṇubhāve /
gandhesu vā candaṇam āhu seṭhaṃ | evaṃ muṇīṇaṃ apaḍinnam āhu 
jahā sayaṃbhū udahīṇa seṭhe | nāgesu vā dharaṇindam āhu seṭhaṃ /
khoodae vā rasa vejayante | tavovahāṇe muṇi vejayante 
hatthīsu erāvaṇam āhu nāe | sīho migāṇaṃ salilāṇa gaṅgā /

 S/7 kusīlaparibhāsiyajjhayaṇe sattame /

 S/8 vīriyajjhayaṇe aṭhame /
 
duhā veyaṃ suyakkhāyaṃ | vīriyaṃ ti pavuccaī /
kiṃ nu vīrassa vīrattaṃ | kahaṃ ceyaṃ pavuccaī 
kammam ege pavedenti | akammaṃ vā vi suvvayā /
eehiṃ dohi ṭhāṇehiṃ | jehiṃ dīsanti macciyā 
pamāyaṃ kammam āhaṃsu | appamāyaṃ tahāvaraṃ /
tabbhāvādesao vā vi | bālaṃ paṇḍiyam eva vā

itto akammaviriyaṃ | paṇḍiyāṇaṃ suṇeha me 
davie bandhaṇummukke | savvao chinnabandhaṇe /

neyāuyaṃ suyakkhāyaṃ | uvāyāya samīhae /
bhujjo bhujjo duhāvāsaṃ | asuhattaṃ tahā tahā 
ṭhāṇī vivihaṭhāṇāṇi | caissanti na saṃsao /
aṇiyae ayaṃ vāse | nāyaehi suhīhi ya 
evam āyāya mehāvī | appaṇo giddhim uddhare /
āriyaṃ uvasaṃpajje | savvadhammam akoviyaṃ 
sahasaṃmaie naccā | dhammasāraṃ suṇettu vā /

evaṃ pāvāiṃ mehāvī | ajjhappeṇa samāhare

 S/9 dhammajjhayaṇe navame /
 
kayare dhamma akkhāe | māhaṇeṇa maīmayā /
añju dhammaṃ jahātaccaṃ | jiṇāṇaṃ taṃ suṇeha me 
māhaṇā khattiyā vessā | caṇḍālā adu bokkasā /

pariggahaniviṭhāṇaṃ | pāvaṃ tesiṃ pavaḍḍhaī /

anne haranti taṃ vittaṃ | kammī kammehi kiccaī 
māyā piyā ṇhusā bhāyā | bhajjā puttā ya orasā /

paliuñcaṇaṃ ca bhayaṇaṃ ca | thaṇḍillussayaṇāṇi ya /

pariggahitthikammaṃ ca | taṃ vijjaṃ parijāṇiyā 
uddesiyaṃ kīyagaḍaṃ | pāmiccaṃ ceva āhaḍaṃ /

āsūṇim akkhirāgaṃ ca | giddhuvaghāyakammagaṃ /
uccholaṇaṃ ca kakkaṃ ca | taṃ vijjaṃ parijāṇiyā 
saṃpasārī kayakirie | pasiṇāyayaṇāṇi ya /
sāgāriyaṃ ca piṇḍaṃ ca | taṃ vijjaṃ parijāṇiyā 
aṭhāvayaṃ na sikkhijjā | vehāīyaṃ ca no vae /
hatthakammaṃ vivāyaṃ ca | taṃ vijjaṃ parijāṇiyā 
pāṇahāo ya chattaṃ ca | nālīyaṃ vālavīyaṇaṃ /
parakiriyaṃ annamannaṃ ca | taṃ vijjaṃ parijāṇiyā 
uccāraṃ pāsavaṇaṃ | hariesu na kare muṇī /
viyaḍeṇa vā vi sāhau | nāvamajje kayāi vi 
paramatte annapāṇaṃ | na bhuñjejja kayāi vi /
paravatthaṃ acelo vi | taṃ vijjaṃ parijāṇiyā 
āsandī paliyaṅke ya | nisijjaṃ ca gihantare /
saṃpucchaṇaṃ saraṇaṃ vā | taṃ vijjaṃ parijāṇiyā 
jasaṃ kittiṃ silogaṃ ca | jā ya vandaṇapūyaṇā /
savvaloyaṃsi je kāmā | taṃ vijjaṃ parijāṇiyā 
jeṇehaṃ nivvahe bhikkhū | annapāṇaṃ tahāvihaṃ /
aṇuppayāṇam annesiṃ | taṃ vijjaṃ parijāṇiyā 
evaṃ udāhu nigganthe | mahāvīre mahāmuṇī /
aṇantanāṇadaṃsī se | dhammaṃ desitavaṃ suyaṃ 
bhāsamāṇo na bhāsejjā | neva vamphejja mammayaṃ /
māiṭhāṇaṃ vivajjejjā | aṇucintiya viyāgare

jaṃ channaṃ taṃ na vattavvaṃ | esā āṇā niyaṇṭhiyā

 S/10 samāhiyajjhayaṇe dasame /
 
āghaṃ maīmaṃ aṇuvīi dhammaṃ | añjū samāhiṃ tam imaṃ suṇeha /

buddhe samāhīya rae vivege | pāṇāivāyā virae ṭhiyappā

āhākaḍaṃ ceva nikāmamīṇe | niyāmacārī na visaṇṇamesī /
itthīsu satte ya puḍho ya bāle | pariggahaṃ ceva pakuvvamāṇe 
verāṇugiddhe nicayaṃ karei | io cue se ihamaṭhaduggaṃ /
tamhā u mehāvi samikkha dhammaṃ | care muṇī savvau vippamukke 
āyaṃ na kujjā iha jīviyaṭhī | asajjamāṇo ya parivvaejjā /
nisammabhāsī ya viṇīya giddhiṃ | hiṃsanniyaṃ vā na kahaṃ karejjā 
āhākaḍaṃ vā na nikāmaejjā | nikāmayante ya na saṃthavejjā /

uṇhaṃ ca daṃsaṃ cahiyāsaejjā | subbhiṃ va dubbhiṃ va titikkhaejjā 
gutto vaīe ya samāhipatto | lesaṃ samāhau parivvaejjā /
gihaṃ na chāe na vi chāyaejjā | saṃmissabhāvaṃ payahe payāsu 
je kei logammi u akiriyaāyā | anneṇa puṭhā dhuyam ādisanti /
ārambhasattā gaḍhiyā ya loe | dhammaṃ na jāṇanti vimokkhaheuṃ 
puḍho ya chandā iha māṇavā u | kiriyākirīyaṃ ca puḍho ya vāyaṃ /
jāyassa bālassa pakuvva dehaṃ | pavaḍḍhaī veram asaṃjayassa 
āukkhayaṃ ceva abujjhamāṇe | mamāi se sāhasakāri mande /
aho ya rāo paritappamāṇe | aesu mūḍhe ajarāmare vva 
jahāhi vittaṃ pasavo ya savvaṃ | je bandhavā je ya piyā ya mittā /
lālappaī se vi ya ei mohaṃ | anne jaṇā taṃsi haranti vittaṃ 
sīhaṃ jahā khuḍḍamigā carantā | dūre caranti parisaṃkamāṇā /

saṃbujjhamāṇe u nare maīmaṃ | pāvāu appāṇa nivaaejjā /
hiṃsappasūyāiṃ duhāiṃ mattā | verānubandhīṇi mahabbhayāṇi 
musaṃ na būyā muṇi attagāmī | nivvāṇam eyaṃ kasiṇaṃ samāhiṃ /
sayaṃ na kujjā na ya kāravejjā | karantam annaṃ pi ya nāṇujāṇe 
suddhe siyā jāe na dūsaejjā | amucchie na ya ajjhovavanne /
dhiimaṃ vimukke na ya pūyaṇaṭhī | na siloyagāmī ya parivvaejjā 
nikkhamma gehāu nirāvakaṃkhī | kāyaṃ viussejja niyāṇachinne /
no jīviyaṃ no maraṇābhikaṃkhī | carejja bhikkhū valayā vimukke  tti bemi ||
     || samāhiyajjhayaṇaṃ dasamaṃ 

 S/11 maggajjhayaṇe eyārahame /
 
kayare magga akkhāe | māhaṇeṇaṃ maīmayā /
jaṃ maggaṃ ujju pāvittā | ohaṃ tarai duttaraṃ 
jaṃ maggaṃ -uttaraṃ suddhaṃ | savvadukkhavimokkhaṇaṃ /
jāṇāsi -aṃ jahā bhikkhū | taṃ -o būhi mahāmuṇī 
jai -o kei pucchijjā | devā aduva māṇusā /
tesiṃ tu kayaraṃ maggaṃ | āikkhejja kahāhi -o 
jai vo kei pucchijjā | devā aduva māṇusā /
tesimaṃ paḍisāhejjā | maggasāraṃ suṇeha me 
aṇupuvveṇa mahāghoraṃ | kāsaveṇa paveiyaṃ /
jam āyāya io puvvaṃ | samuddaṃ vavahāriṇo 
atariṃsu tarantege | tarissanti aṇāgayā /
taṃ soccā paḍivakkhāmi | jantavo taṃ suṇeha me 
puḍhavījīvā puḍho sattā | āujīvā tahāgaṇī /
vāujīvā puḍho sattā | taṇarukkhā sabīyagā 
ahāvarā tasā pāṇā | evaṃ chakkāya āhiyā /
eyāvae jīvakāe | nāvare koi vijjaī 
savvāhiṃ aṇujuttīhiṃ | maimaṃ paḍilehiyā /
savve akkantadukkhā ya | ao savve na hiṃsayā 
eyaṃ khu nāṇiṇo sāraṃ | jaṃ na hiṃsai kaṃcaṇa /
ahiṃsā samayaṃ ceva | eyāvantaṃ viyāṇiyā 
uḍḍhaṃ ahe ya tiriyaṃ | je kei tasathāvarā /
savvattha viraiṃ vijjā | santi nivvāṇam āhiyaṃ 
pabhū dose nirākiccā | na virujjhejja keṇa vi /
maṇasā vayasā ceva | kāyasā ceva antaso 
saṃvuḍe se mahāpanne | dhīre dattesaṇaṃ care /
esaṇāsabhie niccaṃ | vajjayante aṇesaṇaṃ 
bhūyāiṃ ca samārambha | tam uddissā ya jaṃ kaḍaṃ /
tārisaṃ tu na giṇhejjā | annapāṇaṃ susaṃjae 
pūīkammaṃ na sevejjā | esa dhamme vusīmao /
jaṃ kiṃci abhikaṃkhejjā | savvaso taṃ na kappae 
haṇantaṃ nāṇujāṇejjā | āyagutte jiindie /
ṭhāṇāiṃ santi saḍḍhīṇaṃ | gāmesu nagaresu vā 
tahā giraṃ samārabbha | atthi puṇṇaṃ ti no vae /
ahavā natthi puṇṇaṃ ti | evam eyaṃ mahabbhayaṃ 
dāṇaṭhayā ya je pāṇā | hammanti tasathāvarā /
tesiṃ sārakkhaṇaṭhāe | tamhā atthi tti no vae 
jesiṃ taṃ uvakappanti | annapāṇaṃ tahāvihaṃ /
tesiṃ lābhantarāyaṃ ti | tamhā natthi tti no vae 
je ya dāṇaṃ pasaṃsanti | vaham icchanti pāṇiṇaṃ /
je ya -aṃ paḍisehanti | vitticcheyaṃ karanti te 
duhao vi te na bhāsanti | atthi vā natthi vā puṇo /
āyaṃ rayassa heccā -aṃ | nivvāṇaṃ pāuṇanti te 
nivvāṇaṃ paramaṃ buddhā | nakkhattāṇa va candimā /
tamhā sayā jae dante | nivvāṇaṃ saṃdhae muṇī 
vujjhamāṇāṇa pāṇāṇaṃ | kiccantāṇa sakammuṇā /
āghāi sāhu taṃ dīvaṃ | paiṭhesā pavuccaī 
āyagutte sayā dante | chinnasoe aṇāsave /
je dhammaṃ suddham akkhāi | paḍipuṇṇam aṇelisaṃ 
tam eva aviyāṇantā | abuddhā buddhamāṇiṇo /
buddhā mo tti ya mannantā | anta ee samāhie 
te ya bīyodagaṃ ceva | tam uddissā ya jaṃ kaḍaṃ /
bhoccā jhāṇaṃ jhiyāyanti | akheyannāsamāhiyā 
jahā ḍhaṃkā ya kaṃkā ya | kulalā maggukā sihī /
macchesaṇaṃ jhiyāyanti | jhāṇaṃ te kalusādhamaṃ 
evaṃ tu samaṇā ege | micchadiṭhī aṇāriyā /
visaesaṇaṃ jhiyāyanti | kaṃkā vā kalusāhamā 
suddhaṃ maggaṃ virāhittā | ihamege u dummaī /
ummaggagayā dukkhaṃ | ghāyam esanti taṃ tahā 
jahā āsāviṇiṃ nāvaṃ | jāiandho durūhiyā /
icchaī pāramāgantuṃ | antarā ya visīyai 
evaṃ tu samaṇā ege | micchaddiṭhī aṇāriyā /
soyaṃ kasiṇam āvannā | āgantāro mahabbhayaṃ 
imaṃ ca dhammam āyāya | kāsaveṇa paveiyaṃ /
tare soyaṃ mahāghoraṃ | attattāe parivvae 
virae gāmadhammehiṃ | je kei jagaī jagā /
tesiṃ attuvamāyāe | thāmaṃ kuvvaṃ parivvae 
aimāṇaṃ ca māyaṃ ca | taṃ parinnāya paṇḍie /
savvam eyaṃ nirākiccā | nivvāṇaṃ saṃdhae muṇī 
saṃdhae sāhudhammaṃ ca | pāvadhammaṃ nirākare /
uvahāṇavīrie bhikkhū | kohaṃ māṇaṃ na patthae 
je ya buddhā atikkantā | je ya buddhā aṇāgayā /
santi tesiṃ paiṭhāṇaṃ | bhūyāṇaṃ jagaī jahā 
aha -aṃ vayam āvannaṃ | phāsā uccāvayā phuse /
na tesu viṇihaṇṇejjā | vāeṇa va mahāgirī 
saṃvuḍe se mahāpanne | dhīre dattesaṇaṃ care /
nivvuḍe kālamākaṃkhī | evaṃ kevaliṇo mayaṃ  ti bemi ||
     || maggajjhayaṇaṃ eyārahamaṃ 
 S/12 samosaraṇajjhayaṇe bārahame /
 
cattāri samosaraṇāṇimāni | pāvāduyā jāiṃ puḍho vayanti /
kiriyaṃ akiriyaṃ viṇayaṃ ti taiyaṃ | annāṇam āhaṃsu cauttham eva 
annāṇiyā tā kusalā vi santā | asaṃthuyā no vitigicchatiṇṇā /
akoviyā āhu akoviyehiṃ | aṇāṇuvīittu musaṃ vayanti 
saccaṃ asaccaṃ iti cintayantā | asāhu sāhu tti udāharantā /
jeme jaṇā veṇaiyā aṇege | puṭhā vi bhāvaṃ viṇaiṃsu nāma 
aṇovasaṃkhā ii te udāhu | aṭhe sa obhāsai amha evaṃ /
lavāvasaṃkī ya aṇāgaehiṃ | no kiriyam āhaṃsu akiriyavāī 
saṃmissabhāvaṃ ca girā gahīe | se mummuī hoi aṇāṇuvāī /
imaṃ dupakkhaṃ imam egapakkhaṃ | āhaṃsu chalāyayaṇaṃ ca kammaṃ 
te evam akkhanti abujjhamāṇā | virūvarūvāṇi akiriyavāī /
je māyaittā bahave maṇūsā | bhamanti saṃsāramaṇovadaggaṃ 
nāicco udei na atthamei | na candimā vaḍḍhai hāyaī vā /
salilā na sandanti na vanti vāyā | vañjho niyao kasiṇe hu loe 
jahā hi andhe saha joiṇā vi | rūvāiṃ no passai hīṇanette /
santaṃ pi te evam akiriyavāī | kiriyaṃ na passanti niruddhapannā 
saṃvaccharaṃ suviṇaṃ lakkhaṇaṃ ca | nimittadehaṃ ca uppāiyaṃ ca /
aṭhaṅgam eyaṃ bahave ahittā | logaṃsi jāṇanti aṇāgayāiṃ 
keī nimittā tahiyā bhavanti | kesiṃci taṃ vippaḍiei nāṇaṃ /
te vijjabhāvaṃ aṇahijjamāṇā | āhaṃsu vijjāparimokkham eva 
te evam akkhanti samicca logaṃ | tahā tahā samaṇā māhaṇā ya /
sayaṃkaḍaṃ nannakaḍaṃ ca dukkhaṃ | āhaṃsu vijjācaraṇaṃ pamokkhaṃ 
te cakkhu logaṃsiha nāyagā u | maggāṇusāsanti hiyaṃ payāṇaṃ /
tahā tahā sāsayam āhu loe | jaṃsī payā māṇava saṃpagāḍhā 
je rakkhasā vā jamaloiyā vā | je vā surā gaṃdhavvā ya kāyā /
āgāsagāmī ya puḍhosiyā je | puṇo puṇo vippariyāsuventi 
jam āhu ohaṃ salilaṃ apāragaṃ | jāṇāhi -aṃ bhavagahaṇaṃ dumokkhaṃ /
jaṃsī visaṇṇā visayaṅgaṇāhiṃ | duhao vi loyaṃ aṇusaṃcaranti 
na kammuṇā kamma khaventi bālā | akammuṇā kamma khaventi dhīrā /
mehāviṇo lobhabhayāvaīyā | saṃtosiṇo no pakarenti pāvaṃ 
te tīyauppannam aṇāgayāiṃ | logassa jāṇanti tahāgayāiṃ /
neyāro annesi aṇannaneyā | buddhā hu te antakaḍā bhavanti 
te neva kuvvanti na kāraventi | bhūyāhisaṃkāi duguñchamāṇā /
sayā jayā vippaṇamanti dhīrā | vinnatti dhīrā ya havanti ege 
ḍahare ya pāṇe vuḍḍhe ya pāṇe | te attao pāsai savvaloe /
uvvehaī logam iṇaṃ mahantaṃ | buddhepamattesu parivvaejjā 
je āyao parao vā vi naccā | alam appaṇo honti alaṃ paresiṃ /
taṃ joibhūyaṃ ca sayāvasejjā | je pāukujjā aṇuvīi dhammaṃ 
attāṇa jo jāṇai jo ya logaṃ | gaiṃ ca jo jāṇai nāgaiṃ ca /
jo sāsayaṃ jāṇa asāsayaṃ ca | jāiṃ ca maraṇaṃ ca jaṇovavāyaṃ 
aho vi sattāṇa viuaṇa ca | jo āsavaṃ jāṇai saṃvaraṃ ca /
dukkhaṃ ca jo jāṇai nijjaraṃ ca | so bhāsium arihai kiriyavāyaṃ 
saddesu rūvesu asajjamāṇe | gandhesu rasesu adussamāṇe /
no jīviyaṃ no maraṇāhikaṃkhī | āyāṇagutte valayā vimukke  tti bemi ||
     || samosaraṇajjhayaṇaṃ bārahamaṃ 

S/13 āhattahīyajjhayaṇe terahame / 
 
āhattahīyaṃ tu paveyaissaṃ | nāṇappakāraṃ purisassa jāyaṃ /
sao ya dhammaṃ asao asīlaṃ | santiṃ asantiṃ karissāmi pāuṃ 
aho ya rāo ya samuṭhiehiṃ | tahāgaehiṃ paḍilabbha dhammaṃ /
samāhim āghāyam ajosayantā | satthāram eva pharusaṃ vayanti 
visohiyaṃ te aṇukāhayante | je āyabhāveṇa viyāgarejjā /
aṭhāṇie hoi bahūguṇāṇa | je nāṇasaṃkāi musaṃ vaejjā 
je yāvi puṭhā paliuñcayanti | āyāṇam aṭhaṃ khalu vañcaittā /
asāhuṇo te iha sāhumāṇī | māyaṇṇi essanti aṇantaghāyaṃ 
je kohaṇe hoi jayaṭhabhāsī | viosiyaṃ je u udīraejjā /
andhe va se daṇḍapahaṃ gahāya | aviosie dhāsai pāvakammī 
je viggahīe annāyabhāsī | na se same hoi ajhañjhapatte /
ovāyakārī ya hirīmaṇe ya | egantadiṭhī ya amāirūve 
se pesale suhume purisajāe | jaccannie ceva suujjuyāre /
bahuṃ pi aṇusāsie je tahaccā | same hu se hoi ajhañjhapatte 
je yāvi appaṃ vasumaṃ ti mattā | saṃkhāya vāyaṃ aparikkha kujjā /
taveṇa vāhaṃ sahiu tti mattā | annaṃ jaṇaṃ passai bimbabhūyaṃ 
egantakūḍeṇa u se palei | na vijjaī moṇapayaṃsi gotte /
je māṇaṇaṭheṇa viukkasejjā | vasumannatareṇa abujjhamāṇe 
je māhaṇe khattiyajāyae vā | tahuggaputte taha lecchaī vā /
je pavvaīe paradattabhoī | gotte na je thabbhai māṇabaddhe 
na tassa jāī va kulaṃ va tāṇaṃ | nannattha vijjācaraṇaṃ suciṇṇaṃ /
nikkhamma se sevai gārikammaṃ | na se pārae hoi vimoyaṇāe 
nikkiṃcaṇe bhikkhu sulūhajīvī | je gāravaṃ hoi silogakāmī /
ājīvam eyaṃ tu abujjhamāṇo | puṇo puṇo vippariyāsuventi 
je bhāsavaṃ bhikkhu susāhuvāī | paḍihāṇavaṃ hoi visārae ya /
āgāḍhapanne suvibhāviyappā | annaṃ jaṇaṃ pannayā parihavejjā 
evaṃ na se hoi samāhipatte | je pannavaṃ bhikkhu viukkasejjā /
ahavā vi je lāhamayāvalitte | annaṃ jaṇaṃ khiṃsai bālapanne 
pannāmayaṃ ceva tavomayaṃ ca | ninnāmae goyamayaṃ ca bhikkhū /
ājīvagaṃ ceva cauttham āhu | se paṇḍie uttamapoggale se 
mayāiṃ eyāiṃ vigiñca dhīrā | na tāṇi sevanti sudhīradhammā /
te savvagottāvagayā mahesī | uccaṃ agottaṃ ca gatiṃ vayanti 
bhikkhū muyacce taha diṭhadhamme | gāmaṃ ca nagaraṃ ca aṇuppavissā /
se esaṇaṃ jāṇam aṇesaṇaṃ ca | annassa pāṇassa aṇāṇugiddhe 
araiṃ raiṃ ca abhibhūya bhikkhū | bahūjaṇe vā taha egacārī /
egantamoṇeṇa viyāgarejjā | egassa janto gairāgaī ya 
sayaṃ sameccā aduvā vi soccā | bhāsejja dhammaṃ hiyayaṃ payāṇaṃ /
je garahiyā saṇiyāṇappaogā | na tāṇi sevanti sudhīradhammā 
kesiṃci takkāi abujjha bhāvaṃ | khuddaṃ pi gacchejja asaddahāṇe /
āussa kālāiyāraṃ vaghāe | laddhāṇumāṇe ya paresu aṭhe 
kammaṃ ca chandaṃ ca vigiñca dhīre | viṇaijja ū savvau āyabhāvaṃ /
rūvehi luppanti bhayāvahehiṃ | vijjaṃ gahāyā tasathāvarehiṃ 
na pūyaṇaṃ ceva siloyakāmī | piyam appiyaṃ kassai no karejjā /
savve aṇaṭhe parivajjayante | aṇāule yā akasāi bhikkhū 
āhattahīyaṃ samupehamāṇe | savvehi pāṇehi nihāya daṇḍaṃ /
no jīviyaṃ no maraṇāhikaṃkhī | parivvaejjā valayā vimukke  tti bemi ||
     || āhattahīyajjhayaṇaṃ terahamaṃ 

 S/14 ganthajjhayaṇe coddahame /
 
ganthaṃ vihāya iha sikkhamāṇo | uṭhāya subambhaceraṃ vasejjā /
ovāyakārī viṇayaṃ susikkhe | je cheya se vippamāyaṃ na kujjā 
jahā diyāpoyam apattajāyaṃ | sāvāsagā paviuṃ mannamāṇaṃ /
tam acāiyaṃ taruṇam apattajāyaṃ | ḍhaṃkāi avvattagamaṃ harejjā 
evaṃ tu sehaṃ pi apuṭhadhammaṃ | nissāriyaṃ vusimaṃ mannamāṇā /
diyassa chāyaṃ va apattajāyaṃ | hariṃsu -aṃ pāvadhammā aṇege 
osāṇam icche maṇue samāhiṃ | aṇosie -antakariṃ ti naccā /
obhāsamāṇe daviyassa vittaṃ | na nikkase bahiyā āsupanno 
je ṭhāṇao ya sayaṇāsaṇe ya | parakkame yāvi susāhujutte /
samiīsu guttīsu ya āyapanne | viyāgariṃ te ya puḍho vaejjā 
saddāṇi soccā adu bheravāṇi | aṇāsave tesu parivvaejjā /
niddaṃ ca bhikkhū na pamāya kujjā | kahaṃkahaṃ vā vitigicchatiṇṇe 
ḍahareṇa vuḍḍheṇaṇusāsie u | rāiṇieṇāvi samavvaeṇaṃ /
sammaṃ tayaṃ thirao nābhigacche | nijjantae vāvi apārae se 
viuṭhieṇaṃ samayāṇusiṭhe | ḍahareṇa vuḍḍheṇa u coie ya /
accuṭhiyāe ghaḍadāsie vā | agāriṇaṃ vā samayāṇusiṭhe 
na tesu kujjhe na ya pavvahejjā | na yāvi kiṃcī pharusaṃ vaejjā /
tahā karissaṃ ti paḍissuṇejjā | seyaṃ khu meyaṃ na pamāya kujjā 
vaṇaṃsi mūḍhassa jahā amūḍhā | maggāṇusāsanti hiyaṃ payāṇaṃ /
teṇeva majjhaṃ iṇam eva seyaṃ | jaṃ me buhā samaṇusāsayanti 
aha teṇa mūḍheṇa amūḍhagassa | kāyavva pūyā savisesajuttā /
eovamaṃ tattha udāhu vīre | aṇugamma atthaṃ uvaṇei sammaṃ 
neyā jahā andhakāraṃsi rāo | maggaṃ na jāṇāi apassamāṇe /
se sūriyassa abbhuggameṇaṃ | maggaṃ viyāṇāi pagāsiyaṃsi 
evaṃ tu sehe vi apuṭhadhamme | dhammaṃ na jāṇāi abujjhamāṇe /
se kovie jiṇavayaṇeṇa pacchā | sūrodae pāsai cakkhuṇeva 
uḍḍhaṃ ahe yaṃ tiriyaṃ disāsu | tasā ya je thāvara je ya pāṇā /
sayā jae tesu parivvaejjā | maṇappaosaṃ avikampamāṇe 
kāleṇa pucche samiyaṃ payāsu | āikkhamāṇo daviyassa vittaṃ /
taṃ soyakārī ya puḍho pavese | saṃkhā imaṃ kevaliyaṃ samāhiṃ 
asmiṃ suṭhiccā tiviheṇa tāyī | eesu yā santi niroham āhu /
te evam akkhanti tilogadaṃsī | na bhujjameyanti pamāyasaṃgaṃ 
nisamma se bhikkhu samīhiyaṭhaṃ | paḍibhāṇavaṃ hoi visārae ya /
āyāṇaaṭhī vodāṇamoṇaṃ | uvecca suddheṇa uvei mokkhaṃ 
saṃkhāi dhammaṃ ca viyāgaranti | buddhā hu te antakarā bhavanti /
te pāragā doṇha vi moyaṇāe | saṃsodhiyaṃ paṇham udāharanti 
no chāyae no vi ya lūsaejjā | māṇaṃ na sevejja pagāsaṇaṃ ca /
na yāvi panne parihāsa kujjā | na yāsiyāvāya viyāgarejjā 
bhūyābhisaṃkāi duguñchamāṇe | na nivvahe mantapaeṇa goyaṃ /
na kiṃcim icche maṇue payāsuṃ | asāhudhammāṇi na saṃvaejjā 
hāsaṃ pi no saṃdhai pāvadhamme | oe taīyaṃ pharusaṃ viyāṇe /
no tucchae no ya vikaṃthaijjā | aṇāile yā akasāi bhikkhū 
saṃkejja yāsaṃkiyabhāva bhikkhū | vibhajjavāyaṃ ca viyāgarejjā /
bhāsāduyaṃ dhammasamuṭhiehiṃ | viyāgarejjā samayāsupanne 
aṇugacchamāṇe vitahaṃ vijāṇe | tahā tahā sāhu akakkaseṇaṃ /
na katthaī bhāsa vihiṃsaijjā | niruddhagaṃ vā vi na dīhaijjā 
samālavejjā paḍipuṇṇabhāsī | nisāmiyā samiyāaṭhadaṃsī /
āṇāi suddhaṃ vayaṇaṃ bhiuñje | abhisaṃdhae pāvavivega bhikkhū 
ahābuiyāiṃ susikkhaejjā | jaijjayā nāivelaṃ vaejjā /
se diṭhimaṃ diṭhi na lūsaejjā | se jāṇai bhāsiuṃ taṃ samāhiṃ 
alūsae no pacchannabhāsī | no suttam atthaṃ ca karejja tāī /
satthārabhattī aṇuvīi vāyaṃ | suyaṃ ca sammaṃ paḍivāyayanti 
se suddhasutte uvahāṇavaṃ ca | dhammaṃ ca je vindai tattha tattha /
āejjavakke kusale viyatte | sa arihai bhāsiuṃ taṃ samāhiṃ  ti bemi ||
     || ganthajjhayaṇaṃ coddahamaṃ 

 S/15 āyāṇiyajjhayaṇe paṇṇarahame /
 
jamaīaṃ paḍuppannaṃ | āgamissaṃ ca nāyao /
savvaṃ mannai taṃ tāī | daṃsaṇāvaraṇantae 
antae vitigicchāe | se jāṇai aṇelisaṃ /
aṇelisassa akkhāyā | na se hoi tahiṃ tahiṃ 
tahiṃ tahiṃ suyakkhāyaṃ | se ya sacce suāhie /
sayā sacceṇa saṃpanne | mettiṃ bhūehi kappae 
bhūehi na virujjhejjā | esa dhamme vusīmao /
vusimaṃ jagaṃ parinnāya | assiṃ jīviyabhāvaṇā 
bhāvaṇājogasuddhappā | jale nāvā va āhiyā /
nāvā va tīrasaṃpannā | savvadukkhā tiuai 
tiuaī u mehāvī | jāṇaṃ logaṃsi pāvagaṃ /
tuanti pāvakammāṇi | navaṃ kammam akuvvao 
akuvvao navaṃ natthi | kammaṃ nāma vijāṇai /
vinnāya se mahāvīre | jeṇa jāī na mijjaī 
na mijjaī mahāvīre | jassa natthi purekaḍaṃ /
vāu vva jālam accei | piyā logaṃsi itthiyo 
itthiyo je na sevanti | āimokkhā hu te jaṇā /
te jaṇā bandhaṇummukkā | nāvakaṃkhanti jīviyaṃ 
jīviyaṃ piṭhao kiccā | antaṃ pāvanti kammuṇaṃ /
kammuṇā saṃmuhībhūyā | je maggam aṇusāsaī 
aṇusāsaṇaṃ puḍho pāṇī | vasumaṃ pūyaṇāsu te /
aṇāsae jae dante | daḍhe ārayamehuṇe 
nīvāre va na līejjā | chinnasoe aṇāvile /
aṇāile sayā dante | saṃdhiṃ patte aṇelisaṃ 
aṇelisassa kheyanne | na virujjhejja keṇai /
maṇasā vayasā ceva | kāyasā ceva cakkhumaṃ 
se hu cakkhū maṇussāṇaṃ | je kaṃkhāe ya antae /
anteṇa khuro vahaī | cakkaṃ anteṇa loṭhaī 
antāṇi dhīrā sevanti | teṇa antakarā iha /
iha māṇussae ṭhāṇe | dhammam ārāhiuṃ narā 
niṭhiyaṭhā va devā vā | uttarīe iyaṃ suyaṃ /
suyaṃ ca meyam egesiṃ | amaṇussesu no tahā 
antaṃ karanti dukkhāṇaṃ | ihamegesimāhiyaṃ /
āghāyaṃ puṇa egesiṃ | dullabheyaṃ samussae 
io viddhaṃsamāṇassa | puṇo saṃbohi dullahā /
dullahāo tahaccāo | je dhammaṭhaṃ viyāgare 
je dhammaṃ suddham akkhanti | paḍipuṇṇam aṇelisaṃ /
aṇelisassa jaṃ ṭhāṇaṃ | tassa jammakahā kao 
kao kayāi mehāvī | uppajjanti tahāgayā /
tahāgayā appaḍinnā | cakkhū logassaṇuttarā 
aṇuttare ya ṭhāṇe se | kāsaveṇa paveie /
jaṃ kiccā nivvuḍā ege | niṭhaṃ pāvanti paṇḍiyā 
paṇḍie vīriyaṃ laddhuṃ | nigghāyāya pavattagaṃ /
dhuṇe puvvakaḍaṃ kammaṃ | navaṃ vā vi na kuvvaī 
na kuvvaī mahāvīre | aṇupuvvakaḍaṃ rayaṃ /
rayasā saṃmuhībhūyā | kammaṃ heccāṇa jaṃ mayaṃ 
jaṃ mayaṃ savvasāhūṇaṃ | taṃ mayaṃ sallagattaṇaṃ /
sāhaittāṇa taṃ tiṇṇā | devā vā abhaviṃsu te 
abhaviṃsu purā dhīrā | āgamissā vi suvvayā /
dunnibohassa maggassa | antaṃ pāukarā tiṇṇe  tti bemi ||
     || āyāṇiyajjhayaṇaṃ paṇṇarahamaṃ 

 S/16 gāhajjhayaṇe sovvasame /

ahāha bhagavaṃṇṇevaṃ se dante davie vosaṭhakāe tti vacce māhaṇe 
tti vā 1 samaṇe tti vā 2 bhikkhu tti vā 3 nigganthe tti vā 4 | 
paḍiāha-bhante kahaṃ nu dante davie vosaṭhakāe tti vacce māhaṇe tti vā 
samaṇe tti vā bhikkhu tti vā nigganthe tti vā | taṃ no būhi mahāmuṇī || 
iti virae savvapāvakammehiṃ pijjadosakalaha abbhakkhāṇa pesunna 
paraparivāya arairai māyāmosa micchādaṃsaṇasallavirae samie sahie 
sayā jae no kujjhe no māṇī māhaṇe tti vacce  
ettha vi samaṇe anissie aṇiyāṇe āyāṇaṃ ca aivāyaṃ ca 
musāvāyaṃ ca bahiddhaṃ ca kohaṃ ca māṇaṃ ca māyaṃ ca lohaṃ ca pijjaṃ ca 
dosaṃ ca icceva jao jao āyāṇaṃ appaṇo paddosaheū tao tao 
āyāṇāo puvvaṃ paḍivirae pāṇāivāyā siā dante davie vosaṭhakāe 
samaṇe tti vacce  
ettha vi bhikkhū aṇunnae viṇīe nāmae dante davie vosaṭhakāe 
saṃvidhuṇīya virūvarūve parīsahovasagge ajjhappajogasuddhādāṇe uvaṭhie 
ṭhiappā saṃkhāe paradattabhoī bhikkhu tti vacce  
ettha vi nigganthe ege egaviū buddhe saṃchinnasoe susaṃjae susamie 
susāmāie āyavāyapatte viū duhao vi soyapalichinne dhammaṭhī 
dhammaviū niyāgapaḍivanne samiyaṃ care dante davie vosaṭhakāe nigganthe 
tti vacce  se evam eva jāṇaha jamahaṃ bhayantāro  tti bemi ||
     || gāhajjhayaṇaṃ sovvasamaṃ
